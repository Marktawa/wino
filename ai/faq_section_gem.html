FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Which credit cards do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also support secure digital payments through standard mobile money platforms and direct bank transfers.

What courier do you use for deliveries?

For local deliveries within the CBD, we use our dedicated in-house delivery team to ensure your furniture arrives safely. For further destinations, we partner with trusted national freight carriers.

How long will it be before I get a refund?

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are typically processed within 3 to 5 business days. Depending on your bank or credit card provider, please allow an additional 2 to 4 business days for the funds to officially reflect in your account.
