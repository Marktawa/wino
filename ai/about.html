About Winteriors

Winteriors is a proudly Zambian furniture manufacturing and retailing company established in 2018.

We are proud to be one of Zambia's significant furniture manufacturing companies, servicing the country at large.

Winteriors is passionate about manufacturing high quality, durable furniture that is comfortable, well priced and modern in design.

We are known and loved for our minimal living and modern stylish designed couches, which are the principle of our belief in addition to customer focus and meeting our client's needs.
